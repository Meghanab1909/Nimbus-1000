Topic: Interest Rate Increase Clauses

Definition:
Interest rate increase clauses allow lenders to raise interest rates under certain conditions.

Why it is risky:
Unexpected rate increases can significantly raise the cost of a loan.

Common red-flag phrases:
- lender may increase interest rate
- rate adjustment clause
- interest rate subject to review

Fair standard:
Any rate adjustment terms should be clearly defined and communicated.

Detection idea for AI:
Clauses allowing arbitrary interest increases should be flagged.
