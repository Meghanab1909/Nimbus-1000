Topic: Hidden Fees in Loans

Definition:
Hidden fees are charges that are not clearly disclosed in loan agreements. These may include processing fees, administrative fees, or service charges.

Why it is risky:
Borrowers may believe they are paying one cost but later discover additional charges that increase the total loan cost.

Common red-flag phrases:
- administrative fee
- processing fee
- service charge
- additional handling fee

Fair standard:
All loan fees should be clearly disclosed in the loan agreement before signing.

Detection idea for AI:
Contracts with multiple unexplained fees or unclear cost descriptions should be flagged.
