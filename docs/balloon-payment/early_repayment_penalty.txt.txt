Topic: Early Repayment Penalty

Definition:
An early repayment penalty, also called a prepayment penalty, is a fee charged when a borrower pays off a loan before the scheduled end date.

Why it is risky:
Borrowers may want to refinance or repay a loan early to reduce interest costs. Penalties discourage this and can trap borrowers in expensive loans.

Common red-flag phrases:
- prepayment penalty
- early repayment charge
- fee for paying off loan early
- penalty for early settlement

Fair standard:
Borrowers should be informed about any penalties for early repayment before signing the agreement.

Detection idea for AI:
Loan agreements mentioning penalties for early repayment should be highlighted for review.
