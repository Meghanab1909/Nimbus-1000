Topic: Loan Rollover Traps

Definition:
Loan rollover occurs when a borrower extends an existing loan by taking a new loan to pay the previous one.

Why it is risky:
This can create a cycle of debt where borrowers continuously refinance loans and accumulate more interest.

Common red-flag phrases:
- rollover loan
- refinance extension
- renewal loan agreement

Fair standard:
Borrowers should be informed about the cost implications of refinancing or rollover options.

Detection idea for AI:
Clauses encouraging repeated refinancing should be flagged.
