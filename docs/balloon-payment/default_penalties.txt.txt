Topic: Default Penalties

Definition:
Default penalties occur when a borrower fails to meet repayment obligations.

Why it is risky:
High penalties can quickly increase debt and make repayment difficult.

Common red-flag phrases:
- default fee
- penalty interest
- acceleration clause
- additional charges after default

Fair standard:
Penalty fees should be reasonable and clearly explained.

Detection idea for AI:
Contracts imposing excessive default penalties should be flagged as high risk.
